Women In Computing is an organization that started in 2014 at UofSC aiming to help women in computing create a space to meet others within their major. WIC helps students with their career needs so we ask you to contribute if you can and it will help students to get more hands-on experience outside of their classes, travel expenses when we take our students to events, professional outings, and access to different tools and software to make learning fun!
