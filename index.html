Women In Computing




Women In Computing at the University of South Carolina is an organization built upon strong women in the computing department. As a club, we work to improve our computing skills while simultaneously networking with fellow members of the club and through other opportunities in the Columbia area.




Our mission of WIC is to help develop computing skills for women at UofSC and provide opportunities and activites to progress skill sets.
